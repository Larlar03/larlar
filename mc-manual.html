The Micro-Celebrity Manual

A preview of the Micro-Celebrity Manual which is a tongue-in-cheek, 10-step user guide on how to achieve social media fame.

The 100-page manual was created as an interactive e-book, as well as an 8x8in ring-bound printed publication with a transparent front and back perspex acrylic cover.

Creative direction, photography and layout design by Lana Gordon
